Network Service

  • Network Service Offering

APN offers an automated port allocation so you can set up your service across CISCO 9332/9372/9396 and ASR8600 physical devices.

You are able to requests Layer 1 & 2 ports here in any of the following Data Centres:
Clayton, VIC
St Leonards, NSW

Once you have setup your network in APN, you will have access to RDN, TID and Clouds, such as IBM SoftLayer, AWS, and Microsoft Azure.

APN also offers the ability to automatically setup Layer 3 connection through the use of an API.

  • Firewall Service Offering
